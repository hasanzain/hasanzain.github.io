Project information

  • Name: OEE Trendline
  • Client: Technip Malaysia

Details

TechnipFMC, a renowned technology provider in the energy sector, excels in delivering comprehensive solutions, products, and services to both traditional and emerging energy industries. Recently, as part of a cutting-edge project, I developed a sophisticated interface for monitoring and evaluating the spindle condition of their CNC machine. The newly designed interface offers real-time insights into the performance of the spindle, enabling operators to promptly identify any potential issues or anomalies. By closely monitoring key parameters and employing advanced analytical techniques, the interface ensures optimal efficiency and reliability of the CNC machine. Furthermore, I also conducted an in-depth analysis of various parameters associated with the CNC machine. By evaluating these parameters, I assessed the overall effectiveness and performance of the machine, allowing TechnipFMC to make informed decisions regarding maintenance, optimization, and potential enhancements. Through this innovative project, TechnipFMC can further enhance their operational capabilities, increase productivity, and maintain their position as a leading technology provider in the energy industry.
